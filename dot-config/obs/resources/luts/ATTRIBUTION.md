# LUT attribution

`agfa_agfacolor_optima_ii_100.png` is a single preset from the **Free LUT Filter
Pack** by Jordan Wages, distributed on the OBS Project forums:

<https://obsproject.com/forum/resources/free-lut-filter-pack.594/>

Only the one preset actually used by the scene collections is vendored here, to
keep the setup self-contained against the upstream pack going offline. All other
presets, and the full pack, remain available at the link above under its
original terms.
